<em>Uncouth</em>

rust road<br>
you can only look<br>
harsh rivulets<br>
trail of wheels<br>
white bike garlanded<br>
work in your ear <br>
a half seashell <br>
life under anthill moon<br>
narrow motion<br>
for the sake of motion<br>
hoof knife stateroom
