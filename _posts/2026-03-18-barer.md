My first full-length book, <i>A Barer Sky</i>, will be published this spring by Winter Editions. I’m tremendously grateful to Matvei for working with me on this for the better part of the last few years. The book contains poems going back to 2012, including selections from my two chapbooks; a long experimental piece for two voices based on Emerson’s "On Self-Reliance"; and some difficult writings from 2024. 

You can read more about it, and also buy it, [here](https://www.wintereditions.net/home/a-barer-sky).

There will be a variety of events to celebrate the launch throughout this year; stay tuned…
