Summer 2021

Annotations #1: While

In September (hopefully) I'll be putting out the first in the Annotations series. Annotations is a print project with diction as its connective tissue. For the first issue, twelve poets contributed work around the Clark Coolidge poem "While." It is my hope to continue the series throughout 2021-2022.

If you are interested in receiving the prompt for Issue 2, please let me know at ss3789990@gmail.com.
